First three words (substrings) of vis-config.txt are treated as:

prepare_delay_ms - time delay with empty (black) window opened (to set screen recording or smth)
sorting_time_ms - time of the animation itself
admire_delay_ms - time delay after which window is automatically closed

The remaining words are ignored. Non-number symbols are ignored so feel free to add symbols like ' for convenience.
